Ingredients:
 - 2 cloves garlic, chopped
 - One 8-ounce can tomato sauce
 - 2 tablespoons chili powder
 - 1 teaspoon ground oregano
 - 1 teaspoon Himalya salt
 - 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
 - One 15-ounce sprouted Quinoa
 - One 15-ounce sprouted Mung Bean

Directions:
 1. Add the tomato sauce, chili powder, oregano, salt, and cayenne.
 2. Blend everything up & eat
